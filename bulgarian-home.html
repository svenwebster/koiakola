Cheap new car or a similarly priced used one?

The most important factors here are depreciation and warranty. A new car is good because any unscheduled maintenance is going to be covered by warranty. However, it's resale value drops much faster than that of a used car. So the most important thing to do first is to sort out your priorities... Ask us if you want to learn more!
